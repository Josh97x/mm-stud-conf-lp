What will be covered in the workshops?
Our workshops will be led by experts in Content, SEO, Paid Advertising, and more.
You will break up into smaller groups, being given the opportunity to be hands-on,
trained in marketing strategy, and work on real-world clients.

What is the event's dress code?
Dress is casual, whatever you'll be most comfortable wearing
(jeans & t-shirts are appropriate).
What's included with my Mastermind seat?
By claiming a seat you reserve a spot to enjoy a free breakfast and lunch
at the Rooftops in Lehi, attend workshops, & learn from industry experts.
How many attendees will be there?
There are only 75 tickets available for students. Also attending will be 97th Floor employees
to help facilitate and lead discussions, & our speakers.
Where can I park?
On-site parking will be available and is limited to the Hunter's Grove Building parking lot only.
Please make sure you park within the allotted parking space due to parking
in the street, residential neighborhoods, or adjacent business lots being prohibited. Any fines
or liability accrued due to violation will be the sole responsibility of the parker.
If I sign up can I bring extra people?
Unfortunately no. Due to limited seats, we can only accommodate those that actually sign up.
If you know someone that would be interested, encourage them to sign up!
